1. Introduction

MHOERS (Municipal Health Office Electronic Referral System) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our healthcare referral system.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, address, date of birth, and identification numbers
  • Health Information: Medical history, symptoms, diagnoses, and treatment records
  • Professional Information: License numbers, certifications, and professional qualifications
  • Account Information: Username, password, and account preferences
  • Usage Data: System logs, access times, and feature usage

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing healthcare referral services
  • Managing patient records and medical histories
  • Facilitating communication between healthcare providers
  • Improving system functionality and user experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Generating reports and analytics for healthcare management

4. Data Processing Consent

By using MHOERS, you consent to the processing of your personal and health information for healthcare service delivery. This includes:

  • Storage of your data in secure databases
  • Sharing information with authorized healthcare providers
  • Processing data for referral management and care coordination
  • Using anonymized data for system improvement and analytics

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure backup and recovery procedures
  • Staff training on data protection

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Authorized healthcare providers involved in your care
  • Healthcare facilities and institutions
  • Government health agencies as required by law
  • Service providers who assist in system operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing (may affect service availability)
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide healthcare services and comply with legal obligations. Medical records may be retained longer as required by healthcare regulations.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your login session and improve system functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes and update the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the system after changes constitutes acceptance.
